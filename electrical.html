Project description

This is one of the various freelance projects I have been doing in the area of lighting systems. The project started by the AutoCAD electric drawing design. I then discussed with the client who was the architect of the project on how we were going to achieve our objectives.

Challenges

  • Design changed in the process since we figured out that we were to include surveillance systems
  • Security systems were consuming a lot of power and we had to change some elements, we had to include photocell and motion sensors to simplify the operation

Achievements

  • Installed the lighting systems to client satisfactions
  • Installed surveillance camera and we linked them with the owners phone, since he is outside the country he can monitor the house when he feels
  • Power consumptions was greatly reduced after we changed the design and substituted some of the lights with solar lights
